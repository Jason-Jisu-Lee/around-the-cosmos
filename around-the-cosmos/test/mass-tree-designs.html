Mass Upgrade Tree — shared spine + tier gating

A new 8-tier orbiter-unlock spine (working name “Event Horizon”) runs down the left of every tab. Buying a spine level grows Maw and captures one more orbiter — and unlocks that tier across all four tabs. So a category's deeper upgrades stay locked until you've expanded the Maw far enough. Naming the spine

Working title in this demo: Event Horizon — the void's boundary swells, drawing one more body into orbit each level. Other candidates that imply Maw's size expansion: Gravity Well · Maw's Reach · The Expanse · The Widening · Accretion Radius. Each level = +1 orbiter and unlocks the matching tier in all tabs (toward the eventual 12 orbiters).
