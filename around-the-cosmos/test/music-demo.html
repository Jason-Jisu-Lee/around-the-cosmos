Two directions for the next background track. Both loop endlessly, like the in-game three.
Click a card's button to start; starting one stops the other.
Candidate 1 · ambient-leaning
Aphelion
The far point of an orbit. Four wide, cold chords in A minor drift past over sixty-four seconds; a faint music-box motif surfaces on the second and fourth chord, then sinks back into the wash. Sits behind everything and asks for nothing.
Built from detuned sine pads with a soft triangle octave, deep sub roots, sparse plucks, and a long five-second convolution wash. Closest kin: Drift.
Candidate 2 · gently melodic
Perigee
The near point. A soft kalimba arpeggio circles Am, F, C, G at a slow walking pace, and every other pass a quiet melody rises to answer it. Underneath, a low heartbeat lands on the bar, a nod to the Maw's pulse.
Built from lowpassed triangle plucks, a sine lead with light vibrato, sine bass, a felt heartbeat, and a roomy two-second convolution. Closest kin: Celestial.
